Quality Assurance Checklist

Waterproofing (Tanking)

Full "Tanking" System: Liquid rubber/membrane on entire floor + walls (1ft high, 7ft in shower)
Flood Test: 24-48 hours with plugged drains before tiling
Corner Sealing: Specialized tape/mesh at wall-floor junctions

Piping & Drainage

Pressure Testing: All pipes (hot/cold) at 10 bar before wall closure
Acoustic Insulation: Sound-dampening foam on waste pipes
Slope Gradient: 1:100 ratio verification for rapid drainage
Linear Drains: Installation of infinity drains (not center circles)

Concealed Cisterns

Frame Rigidity: Steel frame perfectly rigid (no flex when loaded)
Access Panel: Flush plate allows future mechanism access

Lighting & Electrical

IP65 Rated Zones: Waterproof lights in wet zones (shower)
Niche Lighting: LED strips in shower niches with waterproof diffusers
Toe-Kick Lighting: Motion-sensor LEDs under vanity
CRI Value: High Color Rendering Index bulbs (>90) for accurate grooming

Comfort Technology

Underfloor Heating: Electric mats under tiles with dedicated thermostat
Mirror Defogger: Heating pads behind mirrors
Shaver/Toothbrush Sockets: Hidden inside cabinets or integrated switchplates
Exhaust: High-CFM silent inline fan in false ceiling

Tile Setting Excellence

Layout Dry Run: Ensure no "slivers" (tiny cut pieces) at edges
Mitered Edges: 45¬∞ grinding at corners (no plastic trims)
Lippage Check: Adjacent tiles perfectly flush (no tripping hazards)
Epoxy Grout: Stain-proof, waterproof grout (not cement)

Shower Installation

Thermostatic Valve: Constant temperature regardless of building usage
Rain Shower Head: Ceiling/high-wall mounted with pressure balancing
Hand Shower: Separate wand for cleaning/hair washing

Vanity & Basin

Countertop Support: Steel brackets reinforced for heavy stone
Bottle Trap: Chrome/PVD colored decorative P-trap
Pop-up Waste: Ceramic top plugs matching basin material

WC (Toilet)

Wall-Hung Unit: Mounted 400-420mm off floor
Soft Close Seat: Damper hinges checked for slow closing
Health Faucet/Bidet Spray: High-pressure hose with shut-off valve

Shower Enclosure

Tempered Glass: 8-10mm thickness minimum
Nano-Coating: Hydrophobic treatment for water beading
U-Channels vs. Clamps: Minimalist heavy-duty hardware (no bulky frames)
Magnetic Seals: Watertight door gaskets

Final Snagging ("SOTA" Test)

Silicone Lines: Color-matched to grout, perfectly smooth tooling
Flow Test: All taps + toilet flush simultaneously (check pressure drops)
Drainage Speed: Bucket of water should vanish immediately (no pooling)
